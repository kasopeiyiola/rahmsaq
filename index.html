Brief History

Welcome to Rahmsaq Montessori School

RAHMSAQ SCHOOL: The school was established in 2014, with 9 pupils, 3 teachers and 5 classrooms. The school turn out changed in 2018 but the challenges faced by the school were parents not paying school fees and withdrawing pupils in an improper way.
The school has engaged in some activities like: culture day, excursion, children"s day, career day, colour day, fruit day, and end of session party.

Our Vision

To provide pupils with high integrity and moral values who can compete with other counterparts all over the world.

Our Mission

To provide qualitative islamic and western education.
